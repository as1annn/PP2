140447. Chocolate chip cookies.

Daulet loves chocolate chip cookies very much. He bought a whole box of cookies. He wondered how many chocolate crumbs were in the box? And what is the maximum number of chocolate chips in a box?

The first line of input  contains single integer $n$ --- the number of cookies in the box. $(1 \le n \le 1000)$.

The second line of input contains $n$ space-separated numbers $a_1, a_2, ..., a_n$ --- the amount of chocolate chips in each cookie.  $(1 \le a_i \le 100)$.

Output two space-separated integer --- the total number and the maximum number of chocolate chips in the box.
